Purpose

Supporting the Department of Education in establishing a solid foundation in early learning for disadvantaged young learners.

Services Offered

  • Learning materials development and supply
  • Infrastructure improvements for ECD facilities
  • Accredited & non-accredited training
  • Health, social, and educational support services
  • Poverty alleviation initiatives

About Us

Inkulisa Holistic Resource Unit is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting early education in Buffalo City Metro Municipality. We focus on laying a strong foundation for young learners, especially in rural and peri-urban areas where resources and capacity are critically lacking.

Community Need

At Inkulisa HRU, we recognize the dire situation in our communities. Many rural and peri-urban areas suffer due to a lack of resources, monitoring, evaluation, mentoring, and capacity, which unfortunately impacts the learners' futures and the wellbeing of entire communities. We aim to bridge these gaps and provide much-needed support.

Vision

By 2027, we envision 80% of existing Early Childhood Development (ECD) facilities in the Buffalo City Municipality Metro transformed into world-class establishments, bridging the literacy gap between advantaged and disadvantaged learners.

Mission

We relentlessly provide adequate support aimed at holistically improving learning environments in struggling existing Early Childhood Development (ECD) facilities in the district.
